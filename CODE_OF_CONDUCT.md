# BabelLayer Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We're all here to build something useful and learn along the way. Be cool, be respectful, and let's keep it professional.

## Our Standards

**Supportive behavior:**
* Use welcoming language.
* Be respectful of differing viewpoints.
* Gracefully accept constructive criticism.
* Focus on what's best for the project.

**Unacceptable behavior:**
* Harassment of any kind.
* Trolling or personal attacks.
* Sharing anyone's private information without permission.
